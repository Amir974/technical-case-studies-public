# Publication Notes

This is a curated public case-study artifact, not a supported product.

The examples are synthetic or public-safe composites. They preserve workflow shape, command style, evidence categories, and review boundaries without exposing private data or operational records.

The visual layer includes reviewed diagram PNGs, Mermaid source, and diagram specs. These visuals are included as reviewed public case-study assets.

Claims are qualitative unless separately validated. The artifact should not imply measured performance, reliability, accuracy, adoption, or time savings without a separate evidence check.

The workflow is framed as AI-assisted review, not autonomous action. AI can summarize, explain, evaluate, and identify gaps; final decisions remain human-owned.

Private working material, raw implementation history, operational records, account data, and environment-specific details remain outside this public repository.

Future updates should preserve the same public/private boundary, claim discipline, synthetic-example labeling, and human-review framing.
